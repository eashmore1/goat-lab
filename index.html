How to Play

01
Pick a mode.

Classic shows ratings before you pick. Blind hides them — trust your basketball knowledge.

02
9 rounds, 9 attributes.

Each round assigns you a random team-era and a random attribute: Shooting, Finishing, Handles, Passing, Defense, Rebounding, Athleticism, Clutch, or Height. Pick one player from that roster.

03
Build your GOAT Score.

Your final score is calculated from your nine picks. Elite ratings earn bonuses. Weak spots get penalized. A balanced build gets a bonus. Chase 100.

04
Chase the 100.

Nobody has built a perfect player. Every run is a chance. Can you?
