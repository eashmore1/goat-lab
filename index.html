How to Play

01
Pick a mode.

Classic shows ratings before you pick. Blind hides them — trust your basketball knowledge.

02
9 rounds, 9 attributes.

Each round assigns you a random team-era and a random attribute: Shooting, Finishing, Handles, Passing, Defense, Rebounding, Athleticism, IQ, or Height. Pick one player from that roster.

03
Three respins per round.

Don't love the roll? Each round gives you one Respin Era, one Respin Team, and one Respin Stat — single use each, and they reset every round. Use them to hunt a better matchup.

04
Build your GOAT Score.

Your final score is calculated from your nine picks. Elite ratings earn bonuses. Weak spots get penalized. A balanced build gets a bonus. Chase 100.

05
Chase the 100.

Nobody has built a perfect player. Every run is a chance. Can you?
