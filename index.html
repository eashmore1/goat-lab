How to Play

01
Pick a mode.

Classic shows ratings before you pick. Blind hides them — trust your basketball knowledge.

02
9 rounds, 9 attributes.

Each round assigns you a random team-era and a random attribute: Shooting, Finishing, Handles, Passing, Defense, Rebounding, Athleticism, IQ, or Height. Pick one player from that roster.

03
Respin the era, team, or stat — once each per round.

Don't love the roll? Use your Era, Team, or Stat respin. One of each, and all three reset every new round. Use them freely.

04
Build your GOAT Score.

Elite ratings earn bonuses. Weak spots get penalized. A balanced build gets a bonus. Nobody has scored 100. Can you?
