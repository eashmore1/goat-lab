Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GOAT Lab ("we," "us," or "the game"), operated by Momentum Apps LLC, handles information when you use the game at goat-lab.vercel.app. The short version: we don't collect your personal data.

1. Information we collect

GOAT Lab is a browser-based game that runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require an account, login, or email address to play.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers.
  • We do not run third-party analytics that identify you.
  • Your game progress and builds are not transmitted to us.

2. Hosting and server logs

The game is hosted on Vercel. Like virtually all web hosts, Vercel may automatically log standard technical request data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) for security, performance, and abuse prevention. This data is handled by Vercel under its own privacy practices. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

3. Payments and tips

If you choose to support us through our "Support Us" link, payments are processed by a third-party platform (Ko-fi). We never see or store your full payment details. Any information you provide during a tip is governed by that platform's own privacy policy. Supporting us is entirely voluntary and is not required to play any part of the game.

4. External links

GOAT Lab may link to third-party sites (for example, Ko-fi, X/Twitter, or Momentum Apps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. Children's privacy

GOAT Lab is a general-audience game and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because we collect no personal data, no special collection from children occurs.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date above will be revised accordingly.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email ethan@momentumapps.net.
