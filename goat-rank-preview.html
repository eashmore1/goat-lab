Free vs GOAT Pass

Everyone earns XP and levels up — that's the retention hook that keeps people playing daily.

The GOAT Pass badge becomes the rank chip: the plain 🐐 goes away, and instead pass holders show their gold-bordered rank chip on the public leaderboard. The gold border signals "pass holder," the color & title signal how good they are — one badge doing both jobs, and it levels up over time.

Free players earn XP and have a rank privately, but show no chip on the board — so they watch everyone else flexing theirs. That's what makes them want the Pass.

+ Pass holders earn 25% more XP, so they climb the ranks noticeably faster — another reason the Pass feels worth it. (The exact % is easy to tune.)
